Our Collection

The Archive of Berezowski Family History Institute holds digital copies of thousands of historical documents.

On this page, we present a collection of select, most valuable documents from our archive.

  • Jaroslava Muzyka nee Stefanowicz

    Certificate from the Free Academy of Fine Arts in Lviv confirming her studies from October 1, 1920 to June 23, 1921 in the academy

    Issued in Lviv on July 4, 1921

  • Maxim Muzyka

    Certificate of acceptance to the medical department of the Lviv University

    Issued in Lviv, December 1907

  • Michael Litwinowicz

    Copy of University of Vienna Doctor of Law Diploma

    Issued in Vienna on May 6, 1902

  • Orest Berezowski

    Artists Diploma issued by M. Lysenko High Musical Institute in Lviv.

    Signed by Vasyl Barwinski and Dr. Stanislav Ludkiewicz among others

    Issued in Lviv on March 27, 1938

  • Orest Berezowski

    USSR Supreme Court - Certificate of cancellation of the special settlement sentence, imposed on May 31, 1950 on him and his wife Stefania, effective May 30, 1964

  • Spiridon Litwinowicz

    Announcement of his appointment as The Administrator of Lviv Archeparchy

    Dated January 24, 1858
